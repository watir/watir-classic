Lab Exercises

A series of labs based on the Timeclock application.

  • Lab 1. Getting Started. Test Timeclock manually and develop test ideas.
  • Lab 2. Driving Timeclock Using IRB. Use IRB and the WTR toolkit to drive the Timeclock application.
  • Lab 3. Writing Scripts. Create simple scripts.
  • Lab 4. Verifying Results. Check the expected results in your test scripts.
  • Lab 5. Harnessing Tests. Collect your tests in a test harness. Use assertions.
